Simon Bohnen   is cleared for work at UC Berkeley sites on Monday, September 6th 7:31 AM

Any UC student or employee coming to campus needs to display a ‘green badge’ on the myberkeley app in order to be on campus, which includes a negative symptom screen. Community members may show this certificate instead. See the link to the myberkeley app badge for a convenient way to display your "badge" on your mobile phone.

 

To have a ‘green badge’ you either need to be fully vaccinated and have your records of outside vaccinations uploaded to eTang and have had at least one COVID test on campus. Or if you are unvaccinated or not yet fully vaccinated, you will need to continue weekly testing at a campus testing site. For testing, go to eTang.berkeley.edu, select "Schedule an appointment" in the "Appointments" tab and answer a few questions to get started and select a testing location.
