QuadraSolveTEST

As a voluntary STEM project course in school I developed an iOS app for calculating the real and complex solutions of quadratic equations and optimized its accessibility with blinds using VoiceOver. It already counts thousands of downloads on the App Store and you can read all about the process of buildung it in my German Projektarbeit that has been graded with a 1,0 (~US 4.0). You can find its code open source on GitHub.

AlpakAR coming soon

I'm firmly convinced that Augmented Reality will have enormous impact on the human life and therefore I was keen on getting to know ARKit. As one of the first ones to be awarded with a safe entry to the Hackathon Jugend Hackt (~Youth Hacks), I created a simple iOS app that enables attendees to play in augmented reality, shoot pictures with the Hackathon's mascot (an alpaca) and share them with the community. You can find its code open source on GitHub.

Steering.Remote

In a team of 7 people at the Hackathon Code+Design, organised by CODE University Berlin, we developed an autonomous driving truck based on an Arduino. Together with my friend Simon Bohnen I was responsible of the wireless steering per iOS app. You can find its code open source on GitHub.
